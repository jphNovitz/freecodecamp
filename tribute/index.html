Lisa Leslie was a professionnal basket-ball player.
She has played in Europe at Sicilgesso Sport Club Alamo (Italy). She has played for the Los Angeles Sparks, one of the biggest in Wnba history.

She won the Wnba Championship with the Los Angeles Sparks in 2001 and 2003, each time she was named MPV of the Finals.
On the top of the game she earned mpv titles in 2001, 2004, 2006. She was an All Star Best in 1999, 2001 and 2002.

A day in July 2002 (30), Lisa Leslie became the first woman to dunk the ball in a an offical WNBA game

Lisa was a pionneer in her sport, she won gold medals in 1996 and 2000.

Leslie played with the sparks for 12 years (1997-2009), enough to welcome Candace Parker, her successor in The League

Highlights

  • 363 games with th L.A Sparks, averaging 17.3 points per game
  • 52 playoffs games with Los Angemes, averaging 17.5 points and 9.1 rebounds per game
  • 4 times WNBA MVP (2001- 2004 - 2006)
  • 2 times WNBA Defensive player of the year (2004 - 2008)
  • 8 times All WNBA First Team Member (1997 - 2000 - 2001 - 2002 - 2003 - 2004 - 2006 - 2008
  • 4 times All WNBA Second Team Member (1998- 1999 - 2005 - 2009)
